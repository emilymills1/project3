HISTORY

Feb 12, 2012

Our first gathering was in Brady and Jennafer's house, but the next week we moved into the Summerport Clubhouse.

March 4, 2012

Volunteers have been a crucial part since the beginning! Here are some Blue Shirts preparing to welcome everyone.

April 9, 2012

Serving has been an important part of our mission from the beginning. Here is a group after serving at Coalition for the Homeless.

Nov 4, 2012

This was the first week meeting in the Holiday Inn. We were so excited to be in a place where CP could take the shuttle!

July 4, 2015

We love to host holiday parties to ensure that every Cast Member has friends to celebrate with. This was a fun Independance Day party!

Nov 23, 2016

Turkey Eve was created for us to enjoy community and a great meal the night before Thanksgiving, so they can work on the holiday and let other Cast Members celebrate with their families.

June 8, 2017

Beach Retreat is always a special time in our annual rhythm to draw nearer to jesus and community.

Dec 3, 2018

This was a rally special day to celebrate all that Brady and Jennafer had for this community. Their fingerprints are still on so much of what we do!

Oct 22, 2019

We had prayed for a space to call our own for so many years. It was a special night to get to pray in the building that would be ours in a couple weeks!

Nov 24, 2019

This was our last week meeting in the Holiday Inn. The set up team served so faithfully each week we met there preparing the space.

Dec 1, 2019

This was our first gathering in our own building! It was incredible to be able to celebrate the Christmas season in a place we could call home.

Sept 15, 2020

In a year of so much loss, it was refreshing to be able to still have Beach Day! Our Events Team did a great job finding ways to make this day safe.
